Pocket Ace

Figma

Swift

SwiftUI

Pocket Ace is a multifunctional budgeting app infused with Artificial Intelligence made for students, by students. Pocket Ace considers all the gray areas and misconceptions a student may have when budgeting, such as tuition, housing and transportation. Pocket Ace stands out as it directly partners with universities across Ontario to deliver the most accurate information to students. Pocket Ace has the potential to fill a gap that will lead students into a more financially secure position as they go to university.

Her Path

Figma

Swift

SwiftUI

Her Path is an educational platform designed to close the gender gap in STEM. Her Path aims to give girls aged 13-18 years old the tools to explore STEM subjects, lead and join clubs, and host events. By offering interactive lessons, resources, and access to scholarships, Her Path builds confidence, hands-on skills, and a community where every girl can see her future in STEM.
